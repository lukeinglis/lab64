# Slot Assignments

Character-to-racer slot mappings for the Lab 64 animal pack mod.
See [issue #18](https://github.com/linglis/lab64/issues/18) for the original discussion.

## Assignments

| Animal | Replaces | Weight Class |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Dalmatian | Donkey Kong | Heavy |
| Yellow Lab | Wario | Heavy |
| Black Cat | Luigi | Medium |
| Orange Cat | Bowser | Heavy |

## Rationale

### Weight Classes
SpaghettiKart inherits Mario Kart 64's three weight classes (light, medium, heavy), which affect top speed, acceleration, and off-road handling. Characters inherit the weight class of the slot they replace.

Three of the four animals are assigned to heavy slots (Donkey Kong, Wario, Bowser). The Black Cat takes Luigi's medium slot, providing the team with one faster-accelerating option for technical tracks.

### Visual Distinctiveness
Each animal is visually distinct from the others and from the original character in its slot:

- **Dalmatian** (white with black spots) replaces Donkey Kong — high contrast, easy to spot
- **Yellow Lab** (golden coat) replaces Wario — warm color stands out against most track backgrounds
- **Black Cat** (solid black, green eyes) replaces Luigi — dark silhouette reads clearly at small sprite sizes
- **Orange Cat** (orange tabby) replaces Bowser — bright orange is visible at all zoom levels

### Slot Availability
The four replaced characters (Donkey Kong, Wario, Luigi, Bowser) were chosen because they are not part of the default single-player progression unlocks, minimizing disruption to normal gameplay when the mod is loaded.
